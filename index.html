About Me

I am a final-year Diploma in ICT Technician (Level 6) student at Ramogi Institute of Advanced Technology in Kisumu, Kenya, completing my studies in March 2026.

My passion for technology was shaped by real-world exposure — during my industrial attachment at the ICT Authority of Kenya – Kisumu County Office, I contributed to six major national digital infrastructure projects, from fibre backbone deployment to public WiFi rollout.

My long-term goal is to grow into a globally competitive ICT professional — pursuing a degree while building my career, and eventually contributing to Pan-African digital transformation.
